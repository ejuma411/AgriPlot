This Privacy Policy explains how AgriPlot Connect collects, uses, shares, and protects your information. We are committed to compliance with the Kenya Data Protection Act, 2019.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account details: name, email, phone, and role.
  • Listing data: property details, documents, and pricing information.
  • Usage data: activity logs, notifications, and interactions.

2. Lawful Basis for Processing

We process data based on consent, contractual necessity, legal obligations, and legitimate interests in accordance with the Kenya Data Protection Act, 2019.

3. How We Use Information

  • To verify listings and user identity.
  • To deliver notifications and communication.
  • To improve platform security and performance.

4. Sharing of Information

  • Contact details are shared only for legitimate transactions.
  • We may share data with service providers for email/SMS delivery.
  • We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

5. Data Retention

We retain data as long as necessary to provide services, comply with legal obligations, and resolve disputes.

6. Security

We use access controls, audit logs, and secure storage to protect sensitive data. However, no system is 100% secure.

7. Your Rights Under the Kenya Data Protection Act, 2019

  • Right to be informed about data collection and use.
  • Right to access personal data held by the platform.
  • Right to correction of inaccurate data.
  • Right to deletion in specific circumstances.
  • Right to object to processing in specific circumstances.

8. Data Processing Agreement (DPA) – Summary

Where AgriPlot Connect processes personal data on behalf of a controller, the following terms apply as part of a Data Processing Agreement:

  • Purpose limitation: We process personal data only for documented instructions and verification services.
  • Confidentiality: Personnel with access to personal data are bound by confidentiality obligations.
  • Security measures: We implement safeguards appropriate to risk, including access controls and secure storage.
  • Sub‑processors: We may engage vetted sub‑processors (email/SMS). Where required, we notify controllers of material changes.
  • Data subject rights: We assist controllers with access, correction, deletion, or objection requests.
  • Incident response: We maintain procedures to detect and respond to incidents and notify controllers without undue delay.
  • Retention & deletion: Upon termination or valid request, we delete or return data unless retention is required by law.
  • Audit support: We provide reasonable information to demonstrate compliance.

9. Cookies & Tracking

We may use cookies to improve user experience and site analytics.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use constitutes acceptance of changes.

11. Contact

For privacy requests, contact support.
