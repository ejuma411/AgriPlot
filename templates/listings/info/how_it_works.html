How It Works

AgriPlot Connect uses a structured verification workflow to ensure listings are accurate and trustworthy.

For Landowners & Agents

  1. Create an account and submit a listing.
  2. Upload ownership documents and required identifiers.
  3. Wait for verification stages to complete.
  4. Once approved, your plot becomes publicly visible.

Verification Stages

  • Document review and ownership checks.
  • Extension officer soil and agronomic assessment.
  • Surveyor boundary and access verification.
  • Admin review and publication.

For Buyers

  1. Browse listings and review verification status.
  2. Contact owner/agent directly within the platform.
  3. Request site visits or additional documentation.
