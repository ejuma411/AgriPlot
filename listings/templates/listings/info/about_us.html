AgriPlot Connect is a land listing and verification platform focused on agricultural properties. We help landowners and agents publish credible listings, and help buyers access verified land information.

Our Mission

To improve trust, transparency, and efficiency in agricultural land transactions by combining document verification, field assessments, and price validation.

What We Do

  • Verify ownership and legal documents before publication.
  • Collect extension reports for soil and agronomic suitability.
  • Confirm boundary and access details through licensed surveyors.
  • Provide structured pricing inputs to discourage over‑ or under‑pricing.

Who We Serve

  • Landowners and agents listing agricultural land.
  • Buyers seeking verified land opportunities.
  • Extension officers and surveyors contributing field data.
  • Institutions and partners needing verified land datasets.

Why Trust Matters

Land transactions are high‑value and high‑risk. We enforce structured verification stages and clear records to reduce fraud and disputes.
